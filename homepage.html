Willowbrook Farm (WF) subdivision, situated in the city Novi, is part of Oakland County in the state of Michigan. Willowbrook Farm located on North of 10 Mile Rd /East of Meadowbrook Rd, Novi, is judged as one of the fastest growing city in Michigan and is ranked 32nd among the best places to live in the US.

With access to I-275 / I-96 less than two miles away, Willowbrook Farm is part of the reputed Novi school district, consistently ranked among the top school districts in the US.

Willowbrook Farm is a sprawling habitat, carved out of a wooded landscape, laced by a gentle swirling stream of Bishop creek, unseemingly camouflaged from the din, soaking in tranquil, abundant in flora, enlivens the lingering freshness. We take pride in welcoming you to the community!
